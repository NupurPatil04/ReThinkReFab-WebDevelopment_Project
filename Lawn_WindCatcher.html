Natural Sun Catcher Wind Chimes

Uses

    This is a wonderful upcycling project for old jar lids.
  • You use mason jar lids to create natural sun catchers and when you add them all together, they also make a nice wind chime for the porch or garden.
  • Decorate them with pressed flowers and other natural items and then tie them all up with wire or twine and hang them from a twig or other natural object.

PROCEDURE

Required Material

  • Contact paper
  • Scissors
  • Marker or pen
  • Rings from Mason jar lids
  • String (we used hemp cord – affiliate link – but yarn works too)
  • A sturdy stick

STEPS TO FOLLOW

  • Step 1:Trace mason jar rings onto contact paper and press items onto the sticky side.
  • Step 2:Seal items between two pieces of contact paper, removing air bubbles.
  • Step 3:Cut out circles and poke a hole in each.Attach string to each circle and mason jar ring. Tie the other end of the string onto an old hanger.
  • Step 4:Repeat until all nature rounds have been used.Hang the suncatcher wind chimes on your porch or wherever they can be admired.
