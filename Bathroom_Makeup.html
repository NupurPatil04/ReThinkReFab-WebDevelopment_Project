Makeup Containers

Uses

  • This is an incredibly simple and easy way to reuse empty makeup containers.
  • Some makeup containers, like mascara tubes or lipstick cases, may not be recyclable through regular curbside programs due to their small size or mixed materials.
  • Check if the brand offers a recycling program or consider upcycling them into new containers for DIY beauty products.
PROCEDURE

Required Material

  • Paint
  • Markers
  • Brushes
  • Glue
  • Paper
  • Fabric
  • Stencils
  • Stickers
  • Varnish
  • Tape
  • Scissors
  • Gloves

STEPS TO FOLLOW

  1. Our favorite way to reuse old deodorant tubes is to refill them with DIY deodorant.
  2. DIY deodorant is super easy to make, and you can reuse your old deodorant tube for it too!
  3. Start by emptying your tube of any remaining deodorant by twisting the tube all the way up, and using a spatula or blunt knife to scrape off any remaining bits.
  4. Then twist the tube all the way down, melt your DIY deodorant, and then pour!
