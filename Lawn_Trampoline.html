Old Trampoline

Uses

  • Turn those old garden stones into beautiful birdhouses and hang them throughout your flower bed or lawn.
  • Not only is this a great way to get those stones out of your garden and yard, it’s a wonderful way to treat your little birdies to their own beautiful houses.
PROCEDURE

Required Material

  • Rope with hand splices
  • Padlock chain
  • Old trampoline,without legs
  • Jigsaw
  • Fabric(fleece)
  • Insulation foam
  • Cushions and blankets

STEPS TO FOLLOW

  • Step 1:Cut the fabric and wrap around the outer metal frameof the trampoline.
  • Step 2:Cut insulation foam and bolster the trampoline with the foam.
  • Step 3:Cover the foam with a soft throw,secure by tucking under the edge of the foam.
  • Step 4:Thread ropes around the outer metal frame and hang by using padlock chain.Hook the rope's cabiners into the padlock chain.
  • Step 5:Hang sheer curtains from the top and decorate with lounge cushions and blankets.
