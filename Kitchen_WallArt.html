WALL ART

Uses

  • Decoration: Enhances aesthetic appeal, adds charm.
  • Organization: Neatly arranges items, reduces clutter.
  • Storage: Holds items securely, maximizes space.
  • Display: Showcases objects beautifully, catches attention.
  • Ornament: Embellishes space, adds visual interest.
PROCEDURE

Required Material

  • Cutting Board
  • Paint
  • Brushes
  • Markers
  • Glue
  • Paper
  • Fabric
  • Ribbon
  • Stencils
  • Stickers
  • Varnish
  • Scissors

STEPS TO FOLLOW

  1. Clean the Cutting Board: Ensure the cutting board is clean and free from any dirt or residue.
  2. Arrange Materials: Decide on the arrangement or pattern you want for your wall art using the available materials.
  3. Prepare the Backing: Gather a sturdy backing material such as a board or frame to attach the materials onto.
  4. Attach Materials: Use glue or other appropriate methods to securely attach the materials to the backing in your desired arrangement.
  5. Let Dry: Allow the glue to dry completely before handling the wall art.
  6. Hang or Display: Once dry, hang your wall art on the wall or display it in your desired location.
