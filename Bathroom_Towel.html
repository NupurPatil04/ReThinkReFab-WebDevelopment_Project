Doormats Using Towels

Uses

  • Decoration: Enhances aesthetic appeal, adds charm.
  • Display: Showcases objects beautifully, catches attention.
  • Ornament: Embellishes space, adds visual interest.
PROCEDURE

Required Material

  • Markers
  • Brushes
  • Glue
  • Paper
  • Fabric
  • Ribbon
  • Stencils
  • Stickers
  • Varnish
  • Tape
  • Scissors
  • Sewing machine

STEPS TO FOLLOW

  1. The easiest way to do this is by laying a pair of same-sized towels over one another and stitching the edges together.
  2. Try binding the seams with narrow pieces of fabric for strength and support.
  3. You could use different colors in order to make the coolest bath rug that matches with the rest of your bathroom.
  4. Try making a patchwork rug by cutting towels into squares or stripes and sewing them together.
  5. Another option is to cut towels into 3 in (7.6 cm)-wide strips, then use them to create a circular mat. Stack 3 strips, sew them together at one end, and pin each strip so they are like tubes
  6. Twist the tubes around one another until the mat is the size you want.
