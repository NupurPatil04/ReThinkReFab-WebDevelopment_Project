Seed Starter

Uses

  • Gardening: Initiates seed germination for transplanting.
  • Season Extension: Allows early indoor planting.
  • Experimentation: Enables testing of different growing conditions.
  • Gift Giving: Creates personalized gardening gifts.
PROCEDURE

Required Material

  • Egg Tray
  • Soil
  • Seeds
  • Watering Can
  • Sunlight
  • Monitoring Tools
  • Pots
  • Labels
  • Gloves
  • Trowel
  • Fertilizer
  • Gardening Gloves

STEPS TO FOLLOW

  1. Clean the Egg Tray: Ensure the egg tray is clean and free from any dirt or residue.
  2. Arrange Seeds: Decide on which seeds you want to plant in each compartment of the egg tray.
  3. Fill with Soil: Fill each compartment of the egg tray with potting soil, leaving some space at the top for planting seeds.
  4. Plant Seeds: Plant seeds in the soil-filled compartments of the egg tray according to the planting instructions for each type of seed.
  5. Water: Gently water the soil in each compartment of the egg tray, ensuring it is evenly moist but not waterlogged.
  6. Place in Sunlight: Position the egg tray in a location that receives adequate sunlight for seed germination and growth.
