SeedStarters Using ToiletPaper Rolls

Uses

  • This is an incredibly simple and easy way to reuse toilet paper tubes.
  • Even if you’ve already started your seeds, remember this one for next year!
  • Storage: Holds items securely, maximizes space.
  • Display: Showcases objects beautifully, catches attention.
  • Ornament: Embellishes space, adds visual interest.
PROCEDURE

Required Material

  • Paint
  • Markers
  • Brushes
  • Glue
  • Paper
  • Fabric
  • Stencils
  • Stickers
  • Varnish
  • Tape
  • Scissors
  • Gloves

STEPS TO FOLLOW

  1. Take a tube and make 4 cuts all round one end of the tube.
  2. Fold each strip inwards to form the bottom of the seed pot, then fill with soil and plant your seeds.
  3. Decide on the design or theme you want for your decorative glass container.
  4. Because the cardboard is biodegradable, you can put the pots directly into the ground
  5. when you’re ready to plant, but before you do so, make a couple of cuts along the side of the pot so that the roots can spread out into the garden soil.
