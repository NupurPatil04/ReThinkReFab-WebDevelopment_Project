Recycled Tire Wishing Well

Uses

  • There are so many ways to use tires in your garden décor and this wishing well is probably one of my favorites.
  • It looks really easy to make, too. You’ll need three tires plus the materials to build the top – pool noodles work wonderfully as roofing materials.
  • When this is finished, it’s a beautiful piece to add to your garden and you won’t even have to spend much making it if you already have a few tires on hand.

PROCEDURE

Required Material

  • Used tires
  • Spray paint (color of your choice)
  • plywood
  • Screws,Hinges
  • Power drill
  • Jigsaw
  • Small paintbrush
  • Wood glue

STEPS TO FOLLOW

  • Step 1:Paint your tire,creating the illusion of bricks with white lines.
  • Step 2:Cut two square holes in each tire, aligning them vertically. These will be used for inserting the roof supports.
  • Step 3:Attach the tires together using a tube of landscaping adhesive glue.For the roof, you can use PVC pipes or any other materials you have on hand, such as plastic bottles. Slide the roof down into the holes cut in the tires.
  • Step 4:Optionally, you can stack concrete or add pebbles around the tire to make it more solid. Fill the tires with potting soil/dirt and plant flowers in the top.
